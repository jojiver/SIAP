We provide healthy food for your health.

A small local food business located in Purok 2, Gabi, Cordova, Cebu. The business aims to provide affordable, freshly cooked, and high-quality Filipino dishes that cater to the daily food needs of the community. It offers a variety of meals such as utan (vegetable soup), monggos (mung bean stew), sinabawan (soup dishes), fried chicken, hotdogs, and other well-loved Filipino delicacies suitable for everyday dining.
